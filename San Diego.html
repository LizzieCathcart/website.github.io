Trip to San Diego!

July 2016

Day 1

So we headed off to San Diego for a 5 day trip to visit with the Cloyds who were having a wedding celebration for Sam and Becca. Our flight was to leave Newark at 10:30 with a layover in Chicago. It turned out the the layover in Chicago was for 6 hours!!

We landed in San Diego safe and sound, but late (at least it felt late for us North-Easterners), and we were starving. So off we headed to the highly spoken about In and Out Burger, which turned out sharing a parking lot with our hotel. Convenient and delicious!

Poor Eamon was pretty exhausted.

Day 2

We had a nice suite at the Holiday Inn which slept 7 of us, had a great breakfast buffet, and a swimming pool. Oh, did I mention that it shared a parking lot with In and Out Burger? Or that it was only a two minute drive to Starbucks?

We headed over to the cloyd's beautiful home to help them get ready for the party. They ordered special popsicles; which the girls helped map out for the guests.

The pool was magnificent, as was the weather.

Also, the Cloyd's rented a huge slip and slide for the party, which went beautifully with the scenery.

Nellie and I!

In the afternoon, the guests started showing up for the party, and a Taco Truck came to serve dinner. Andy and Jennifer May came, along with Michael and Lindsay.

The Cathcart-Cloyds, and Burke

The Cloyds commented that it was one of the best parties that had ever thrown!
